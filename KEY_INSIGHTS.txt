LIMITATIONS & CONSIDERATIONS
===============================================================================

### Data Limitations

1. **Geographic Scope:**
   - Only Jawa Timur
   - Cannot generalize to Indonesia
   - Regional differences not captured

2. **Temporal Granularity:**
   - Monthly aggregation
   - Daily/weekly patterns not visible
   - Seasonal variations might be smoothed

3. **Outliers:**
   - 9.48% capped
   - Extreme values information lost
   - Might miss important anomalies

4. **Attribution:**
   - Don't know WHY outliers occurred
   - Cannot attribute to specific causes
   - Need qualitative data for full picture

### Analysis Limitations

1. **Correlation vs Causation:**
   - Correlation analysis shows relationships
   - Doesn't prove cause-effect
   - Need controlled studies for causation

2. **Aggregation Level:**
   - Data is aggregated
   - Individual patient level not available
   - Cannot do patient-centric analysis

3. **External Factors:**
   - Economic conditions not included
   - Policy changes not tracked
   - Environmental factors unknown
